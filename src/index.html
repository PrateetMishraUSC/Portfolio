About me

Hi, I’m Prateet Mishra, a Software Developer and ML/AI Engineer currently pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science at the University of Southern California. I’m passionate about building scalable, intelligent systems that merge engineering precision with real-world impact. Over the past few years, I have developed AI-driven products, such as SmartMedAI (a fine-tuned medical Q&A LLM) and WeBudget (an AI-powered expense-sharing platform), and engineered real-time web applications using technologies like Next.js, React, Node.js, Prisma, and TensorFlow. My work reflects a deep curiosity for how design, data, and distributed systems intersect, whether it’s optimizing an LLM with reinforcement learning or crafting a seamless full-stack user experience.
